Analytics Club Founding

Description

Founded the Govt. Titumir College Analytics Club in January 2023, growing it to over 2,000 members. The club fosters collaborative learning in data analysis through workshops, seminars, and hands-on projects, empowering students to develop analytical skills and engage with real-world data challenges.

Impact

Created a vibrant community for data enthusiasts, organized 10+ workshops on tools like R and Python, and facilitated networking opportunities with industry professionals, enhancing students’ career prospects in analytics.

Research Proposal Writing Contest

Description

Won 1st Place in the Research Proposal Writing Contest at Govt. Titumir College in December 2023. The winning proposal focused on an innovative study design addressing public health challenges, recognized for its clarity, feasibility, and academic rigor.

Impact

The award highlighted my ability to design impactful research, earning recognition from faculty and peers, and inspiring further exploration of public health research topics.

StatQuery YouTube Channel

Description

Created and managed the StatQuery YouTube channel from January 2023 to December 2023, producing educational content on statistical concepts, data analysis techniques, and software tools like R and SPSS, aimed at undergraduates and aspiring data analysts.

Impact

Reached over 1,000 viewers, simplifying complex statistical topics and providing practical tutorials, which enhanced students’ understanding and application of data analysis in academic and professional settings.
